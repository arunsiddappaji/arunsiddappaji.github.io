    Testing

     

    Understanding Testing from different perspective!!!!!

     

    1. Why Testing?
    • Get to know why do we need testing, what is the impact of not performing testing on any product

     

    1. Testing Methodologies
    • Get to know how to start testing, what to test and how to be very sure you are delivering good quality product, understand different testing methodologies and Software Development Life Cycle from a practical perspective

     

    1. Test Plan
    • "Failing to Plan? Then you’re planning to Fail" that's the importance of planning. Get to know how test planning is done and how to make best test plan ensuring good quality of the product

     

    1. Test Case Development
    • Get to know what is a test case, importance of test case, how to design effective test cases no matter what product you are testing

     

     

     

     

     

     

    1. Problem Tracking and Reporting
    • Get to know how to report and track bugs that you file during testing. Get to know the importance of

     

    reporting, effective reporting and tracking to make sure overall health of the product and organization, yes organization

     

    1. Types of Testing
    • Get to know different types of testing you can perform on a product. Choose the right type of tests for each product.

     

    1. Testing Tools
    • Get to know some unique and effective tools used in systems domain testing.

     

    1. Automation
    • Get to know what is automation role in the area of testing, how important it is, how to perform automation testing, in this you happen to learn some scripting language to make automation testing effective.

     

     

    1. Practice Sessions
    • Apply the above all learning’s in a practical scenario and make yourself expert in the area of testing
